Results

This is our list for how to fill up your car at a gas station

  • Locate nearby gas stations.
  • Compare their prices, availability of gas pumps, and the gas station’s overall appearance (Is it shady-looking? If it is, pick a different option.)
  • Drive up to a pump with your car’s gas tank close to the gas pump.
  • Park the car, then turn off its engine.
  • Follow the instructions on the gas pump’s screen.
  • It will ask you to pre-authorize a payment method before selecting a fuel.
  • After payment, select the appropriate fuel premium for your car.
  • Open your car’s gas tank. You can usually do this by pressing down the cover, lifting it open, and twisting off the cap.
  • Take the nozzle from the gas pump.
  • Insert nozzle into the gas tank.
  • Hold down the trigger on the nozzle to release fuel into your car.
  • The nozzle will automatically stop fueling your car once the tank is full.
  • Remove the nozzle from your car and return it to the gas pump.
  • Screw the cap on your gas tank back in, usually until you hear a click, and then close the cover.
  • Take your receipt from the gas pump.
  • Get in your car and reset the odometer.
  • Ensure your dash’s fuel display correctly reflects that you’ve filled the tank.
  • If this is good to go, you’ve finished fueling your vehicle.
